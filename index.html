WebRTC for the Streamer

WebRTC for the Streamer was written by the developers who added WHIP/WebRTC support to OBS and maintain Broadcast Box. We hope WHIP and WebRTC make streaming more accessible and more connected. All of this work is non-profit and open source.

This site is a companion piece to WebRTC for the Curious and Pion.
