WebRTC for the Streamer

We wrote this site to explain/share the ways that we believe WebRTC (WHIP/WHEP) can improve streaming. Things like improving quality, making it easier to self and reducing latency. Some of these changes may only benefit a niche of streamers, but to those streamers it is a dramatic improvement!

WebRTC for the Streamer was written by the developers who added WHIP/WebRTC support to OBS and maintain Broadcast Box. A companion piece to this site is WebRTC for the Curious.
